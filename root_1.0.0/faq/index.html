Frequently Asked Questions

To recover your account, navigate to the login page and click “Forgot Password.” Enter the email address associated with your account, and you’ll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Follow the link to set a new password.

After logging in, go to “My Orders” in your account dashboard. You’ll see a list of all your recent orders. Click on any order to view detailed status updates, tracking numbers, and estimated delivery dates.

If you experience any technical issues, first try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. If the problem persists, submit a support ticket with “Technical Issue” as the subject, including screenshots and detailed steps to reproduce the error.

Coinit accepts major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), as well as PayPal. Depending on your location, you may also have the option to pay via bank transfer or local e-wallet services.

To update your shipping address, go to “Account Settings” and select “Address Book.” From there, you can add a new address or edit your existing one. Remember to save your changes before placing a new order.

If you lose access to your authenticator app, submit a request through our support form with “2FA Reset” in the subject. Provide account details and any backup codes. Our security team will verify your identity before disabling 2FA.

Refunds are issued within 7 business days for eligible products returned in their original condition. To request a refund, contact support with your order number and reason for return. Digital goods are non-refundable unless defective.

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can click the tracking link provided or enter the number on the carrier’s website to see real-time updates on your delivery status.

To update payment information, go to “Billing & Payments” in your account settings. Click “Edit” next to your saved card, enter the new details, and click “Save.” Future purchases will use the updated card.

If your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery date, first check the tracking status. If there’s no update after 3 days, submit a support ticket with your order number, and we’ll investigate with the carrier on your behalf.

To permanently delete your account, go to “Account Settings” and select “Delete Account.” You will be prompted to confirm your decision. Note that this action is irreversible and you will lose access to all order history and data.

Orders can be modified within 30 minutes of placement. Contact support immediately with your order number and requested changes. After 30 minutes, orders may be in processing and cannot be guaranteed for modification.

KYC (Know Your Customer) is a verification process to confirm your identity. To complete it, upload a valid government-issued ID (passport or driver’s license) and a selfie. Our team will review your documents within 24–48 hours.

Payment can be declined for several reasons: incorrect card details, insufficient funds, or bank restrictions on online transactions. Verify your information, ensure your card is active for international use, and try again. If it still fails, contact your bank.

Enter your discount or promo code at checkout in the “Promo Code” field and click “Apply.” The total price will update to reflect the discount. Promotions cannot be combined, and each code has its own expiration date and terms.

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping times and rates vary by destination. During checkout, enter your address to see available shipping options and estimated delivery times for your region.

To cancel, go to “Subscriptions” in your account settings and click “Cancel Subscription.” You’ll continue to have access until the end of your current billing cycle. No partial refunds are provided for unused time.

Login to your account, go to “Orders & Billing,” then click on the relevant order. You’ll see an option to “Download Invoice” or “View Receipt.” Click it to download a PDF copy for your records.

To change your email, go to “Account Settings” and select “Edit Email.” Enter your new email address and current password, then click “Save Changes.” A confirmation link will be sent to the new email—click it to finalize the update.
