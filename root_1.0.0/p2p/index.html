How P2P Trading Works

  • 1. Choose an Offer: Browse available buy/sell offers and select one that meets your price and limit criteria.
  • 2. Initiate Trade: Click “Trade” to open a secure trade window where you can chat with the counterparty.
  • 3. Transfer Funds: Follow the seller’s payment instructions (e.g., Bank Transfer, PayPal). Confirm payment has been made.
  • 4. Confirm Receipt: Once the seller confirms receipt of your payment, the cryptocurrency will be released to your P2P wallet.
  • 5. Leave Feedback: After completing the transaction, rate and review your trading partner to help maintain trust within the community.
