Trading Fees

Below is a comparative view of maker and taker fees across Binance, Coinbase, and Kraken. Fees are based on 30‑day rolling trading volume in USD. Discounts such as BNB usage (Binance) or promotional rebates (Coinbase) are noted in the remarks.

30‑Day Volume (USD) Maker Fee (%) Taker Fee (%) Remarks
Up to 50,000 0.10% 0.15% Base rate; 25% discount with BNB → 0.075% maker, 0.1125% taker
50,000 – 100,000 0.09% 0.14% 50 k tier; BNB discount applies
100,000 – 250,000 0.08% 0.13% 100 k tier; BNB discount applies
250,000 – 500,000 0.07% 0.12% 250 k tier; BNB discount applies
500,000 – 1,000,000 0.05% 0.10% 500 k tier; BNB discount applies
Above 1,000,000 0.02% 0.04% VIP 0; BNB discount applies

Source: Binance Fee Schedule (accessed May 2025).

10,000 – 50,000 0.35% 0.60% Tier 2; 30 day volume ≥ 10 k
50,000 – 100,000 0.25% 0.40% Tier 3
